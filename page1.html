What is Robotics?

Robotics is a multidisciplinary field involving the design, construction, operation, and use of robots. Robotics incorporates elements of mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, computer science, artificial intelligence, and more.

From assembling products in factories to performing surgery or exploring distant planets, robotics is transforming industries around the world.
