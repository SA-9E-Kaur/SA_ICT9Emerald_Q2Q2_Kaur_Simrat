AI and Machine Learning in Robotics

Artificial Intelligence (AI) plays a major role in enhancing the capabilities of robots. Machine learning algorithms allow robots to learn from their experiences, improving their performance over time.

AI allows robots to perform tasks autonomously, from manufacturing to healthcare applications, making them smarter and more efficient.
